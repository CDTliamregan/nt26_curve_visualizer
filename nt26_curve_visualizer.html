Joint range −7.5° to +10° in 2.5° steps. Top-down plan view.

Radius assumes every joint is set to the same angle. The dashed circle is the curve the wall sits on; the orange segments are the panel faces with joints between them. "Chord" is the straight-line distance end to end; "arc depth" is how far the curve bulges from that line — useful for checking your stage footprint.
